The Idea

The risk of getting Alzhimer's has beenn increasing why we are giving an AI model that helps doctors to predict the disease before the pre-clinical state with different tests such as MRI, PET, Amyloid PET and so on using modells such as Tensorflow and CNN

Core Functionality

NeuroShield uses a multimodal fusion architecture combining three input streams: MRI scans (EfficientNetB4 CNN for spatial atrophy patterns), Amyloid PET scans (ResNet-based extractor for plaque deposits), and blood biomarkers (dense MLP layers for tabular biomarker values). Each stream produces an embedding; these are concatenated and passed through fusion dense layers with dropout regularization. The final softmax output classifies Alzheimer's stage — Normal, MCI, or Dementia — with Grad-CAM overlays for clinical explainability.
